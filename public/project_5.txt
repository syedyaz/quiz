4. What is the fundamental consistency challenge in PMEM file systems, and why is write ordering critical for crash-safety when data can persist across power failures?

8. Explain the implications of PMEM for the virtual memory subsystem: how should the page cache interact with DAX-enabled file systems, and what happens when PMEM pages are mapped directly into user space?

9. What are the challenges of implementing a minimal journaling or undo-logging scheme for PMEM, and how would you evaluate its correctness under various failure scenarios (e.g., crash during write, power loss during flush)?
